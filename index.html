Who We Are

A Community Built on Hope & Compassion

Naye Pankh is a non-profit organization dedicated to transforming the lives of marginalized children and families across India. Founded in 2012, we believe that access to education and nutrition is a fundamental right — not a privilege.

Our team of passionate volunteers works tirelessly in underserved communities, running learning centers, health camps, and skill workshops.
